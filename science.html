Nutritional Values

01

Vitamin C

Oranges are famous for their high vitamin C content, which helps boost the immune system,
promote collagen synthesis, and act as an antioxidant.

02

Dietary Fiber

Oranges contain dietary fiber that aids in digestion and helps maintain a healthy gut.

03

Vitamin A

Oranges are a good source of vitamin A, which supports vision health, immune function,
and cell growth.

04

Potassium

Oranges provide a decent amount of potassium, an electrolyte essential for maintaining
proper heart and muscle function.

05

Folate

Oranges contain folate, a B-vitamin crucial for cell division and the production of DNA and RNA.

06

Thiamin & Vitamin B6

Oranges also contain thiamin and vitamin B6, which play vital roles in energy metabolism.
